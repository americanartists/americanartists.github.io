Support for Individual Artists

by Jules White

How do you get a grant to pursue art?

What agencies give grants to the arts? What forms of non-monetary support are available for artists? If you have any of these questions, we can help. First, it makes sense to read Private Giving to Individual Artists to get an idea of what private support is available. Next, you should look through our listings of private organizations that support the arts and government agencies that support the arts. Finally, take a look at the National Endowment for the Arts and the help they offer.
